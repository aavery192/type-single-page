History

Recursive was created by the studio ArrowType. Recursive was created to explore the new technology of variable fonts and aimed to push the boundaries of what variable fonts can do. Recursive was named after the ideas of recursion and cursive writing. The designer used their interests, experience and user’s needs to create a typeface that creates unique possibilities for interactive design. Recursive has multiple different changeable axes including slant, weight, casual, cursive and monospace. Recursive is a typeface with the versatility of multiple classifications that creates a new meaning and limit for what variable fonts are capable of. Recursive is free for use in any personal or commercial projects.
